Everest Base Camp Trek – 12 Days

Take the journey of a lifetime and trek to Everest Base Camp. Explore stunning landscapes and experience the thrill of reaching the foot of the world's highest peak!Reaching Everest Base Camp at 5,364 meters (17,598 feet) is a monumental achievement, and the views of the Khumbu Icefall and surrounding peaks are absolutely mesmerizing.
